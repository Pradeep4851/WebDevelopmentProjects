Kanaka Durga Temple

Kanaka Durga Temple, officially known as Sri Durga Malleswara Swamyvarla Devasthanam, is a Hindu temple dedicated to Kanaka Durga. The deity in this temple is also popularly referred as Kanaka Durga

Location

The temple is located in Vijayawada, Andhra Pradesh, India on the Indrakeeladri hill on the banks of Krishna River

Taj Mahal

The Taj Mahal is a white marble mausoleum in Agra, India, that was built by the Mughal emperor Shah Jahan to honor his wife, Mumtaz Mahal

Location

The Taj Mahal is on the south bank of the Yamuna River in Agra, India.

Charminar

The Charminar is a historic monument and mosque in Hyderabad, India, that is known for its architecture, history, and culture

Location

The Charminar is located near the Musi river, close to Laad Bazaar and Mecca Masjid. The nearest railway station is Hyderabad Deccan Railway Station (HYB), also known as Nampally Railway Station.

Buddhist Temple

A Buddhist temple or Buddhist monastery is the place of worship for Buddhists, the followers of Buddhism. They include the structures called vihara, chaitya, stupa, wat and pagoda in different regions and languages. Temples in Buddhism represent the pure land or pure environment of a Buddha.

Location

Sanchi Stupa is a Buddhist complex, famous for its Great Stupa in India and it is located, about 23 kilometers from Raisen town, district headquarter and 46 kilometres (29 mi) north-east of Bhopal, capital of Madhya Pradesh.

Ajanta Caves

TThe Ajanta Caves are a group of Buddhist cave temples and monasteries in Maharashtra, India, known for their art and architecture

Location

The caves are located in the Sahyadri Hills, in a forest ravine in the Wagurna River valley, about 100 km northeast of Ellora and 104 km from Aurangabad.

Konarka Temple

Konark Sun Temple is a 13th-century CE Hindu Sun temple at Konark about 35 kilometres (22 mi) northeast from Puri city on the coastline in Puri district, Odisha, India. The temple is attributed to king Narasingha Deva I of the Eastern Ganga dynasty about 1250 CE.

Location

The Temple is located in an eponymous village (now NAC Area) about 35 kilometres (22 mi) northeast of Puri and 65 kilometres (40 mi) southeast of Bhubaneswar on the Bay of Bengal coastline in the Indian state of Odisha.

Agra Fort

The Agra Fort is a historical fort in Agra, India, that was the main residence of the Mughal emperors until 1638. It's a UNESCO World Heritage site and is located on the right bank of the Yamuna River, about 2.5 kilometers northwest of the Taj Mahal.

Location

Agra Fort, large 16th-century fortress of red sandstone located on the Yamuna River in the historic city of Agra, west-central Uttar Pradesh, north-central India.
